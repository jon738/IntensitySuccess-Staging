AI-Assisted Weekly Planning (in 20 Minutes)

Oct 21, 2025 • 6 min read
#ai #systems

Most people over-engineer their weekly planning. They spend hours building templates they never use. The truth? You can run an effective planning session in 20 minutes — and AI can help you do it consistently.

1. Capture everything

Start by brain-dumping tasks, ideas, and loose goals into one place. Use ChatGPT or your AI notes assistant to summarize your inbox, meetings, and open tasks into bullet points.

“Your brain is for having ideas, not holding them.” — David Allen

2. Categorize quickly

Label tasks into three lanes: Deep Work, Admin, and Delegation. Let AI sort them using simple prompts like ‘Classify these tasks by focus level and complexity.’

3. Schedule your focus blocks

Choose two or three deep-work goals for the week. Add them to your calendar first, then fill in meetings and admin around them. Protect those blocks — AI can automate rescheduling when conflicts arise.

4. Automate the low-value layer

Use lightweight automation: inbox triage, recurring task creation, and summaries. The goal is to reduce cognitive overhead, not build an entire system. Let AI suggest improvements weekly.

5. Review and reset

End the week with a five-minute reflection: What worked? What needs adjustment? Feed that into next week’s plan. Over time, your system compounds — you’ll spend less time planning and more time doing.

When AI handles the friction, your job is just to think clearly and act intentionally.
